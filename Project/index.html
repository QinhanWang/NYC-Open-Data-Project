Welcome to the farmers market data website!

OUR GOAL

For this website in particular, users can get access to and learn about Farmer Markets in NYC and their location, year opened, operating hours, and if or if not they accept EBT. Users are able to filter this data based on their preferences and can also access analytics relating to the data.

ABOUT NYC OPEN DATA

NYC open data is a free, public use website with public data from many sources and services in New York City. The data provided comes directly from the city government and is available to any and all users online who want to view a certain piece of information. You can view their website here.
